What is Triangulation?

Trangulation is a method of calculating where a point is using different points and angles.

This website is used for stronghold triangulation, a method used, in speedrunning,
to find the stronghold in Minecraft.

How it Works

The Eye of Ender only shows you what direction the stronghold is. By using your x and z coordiantes in the game or by just being precise, you could calculate where the stronghold is in your world with just two Eyes of Ender.

The Calculator

Just throw your Eye of Ender, mark the direction it went, run perpendicularly away from that direction then, throw your Eye of Ender, mark the direction again, and put the information into the calculator to see how far away you are for that point!
